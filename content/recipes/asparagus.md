**Ingredients**
- Asparagus
- Olive Oil
- Salt
- Pepper
- Parmesan Cheese
- Krauterlinge
- Garlic Salt *(optional)*

**Instructions**
1. Throw it all on!
2. Bake it at 350 for ~30 minutes. It depends how much asparagus is in the pan and how nicely they are spread.
