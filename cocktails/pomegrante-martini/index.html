    Pomegranate Martini

    Ingredients

    • 2oz Basic Vodka
    • 0.5oz Simple Syrup
    • 1oz Pomegranate Juice (0.75oz Pomegranate Juice, 0.25oz Lemon Juice)
    • Lemon Wheel

    Instructions Pour all the ingredients into a cocktail shaker, add ice and shake vigorously. Strain into your favorite martini or rocks glass. Garnish appropriately and enjoy!
