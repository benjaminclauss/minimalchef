    Espresso Martini

    Ingredients

    • 1.5oz Basic Vodka
    • 1oz Espresso
    • 1oz Liquer Mixture (0.5oz Kahlua & 0.5oz Licor 43)
    • 0.25oz Simple Syrup (optional)
    • 3 Espresso Beans

    Instructions

    Pour all the ingredients into a cocktail shaker, add ice and shake vigorously. Strain into your favorite martini or rocks glass. Garnish appropriately and enjoy!
